A driver's license is not required to ride an e-bike. To operate an e-car, you must have a B or B1 category driver's license, and be at least 23 years old. In case of e-moped, you must have an AM, B, B1, or any other valid driver's license.

Parking zones

When you open the Qari app, you'll notice the areas on the map. Each Qari vehicle type has its own color. You can begin and end your journey within this zone.
The red zones marked on the map mean that riding in this area is restricted.
